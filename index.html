What are we doing in Project Overland?

Project Overland is halfway between software and video game, in which users will play multiplayer role-playing games. We want to take RPG players further in their immersions and give them the opportunity to create their own universe. In fact, in Project Overland we're developing a map creation editor to enable players to play in the world of their dreams. Game masters will be able to create their own tailor-made stories
